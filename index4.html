How it works

01

Speech to text

Combining Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for feature extraction and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) for sequential modeling, our speech-to-text system achieves remarkable accuracy. This dual architecture allows us to capture both spatial and temporal dependencies in audio data, providing a robust solution for converting spoken language into accurate and reliable text

02

Text Filtering

Our text filtering technology employs advanced algorithms to swiftly and efficiently remove unwanted content, ensuring a clean and relevant user experience. With a focus on precision, our system effectively filters out noise, allowing only valuable and desired information to surface. Experience streamlined communication without the distraction of irrelevant text.

03

Database

Effortlessly retrieve videos from our database based on text queries. Our system intelligently matches the provided text with corresponding videos, presenting a seamless integration of content and context. Enjoy a personalized viewing experience as the displayed text seamlessly aligns with the retrieved video content, enhancing accessibility and engagement.

04

Skeleton Generation

Revolutionizing video interaction, our system generates a skeleton based on fetched videos, mapping intricate gestures onto a dynamic framework. This innovative approach enhances understanding and engagement by translating real-world movements into a digital skeleton, providing a more immersive and interactive experience. Explore a new dimension of content interaction through our cutting-edge skeleton generation technology.

05

Avatar Generation

Elevating digital presence, our system maps the dynamically generated skeleton onto a customizable avatar. Witness the fusion of cutting-edge technology as real-world gestures seamlessly translate into the avatar's movements, creating a lifelike and personalized virtual experience. Immerse yourself in a new era of interaction where your digital avatar mirrors your authentic expressions with precision and fluidity.
